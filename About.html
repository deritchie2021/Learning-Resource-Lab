About Us

The Learning Resource Lab is a Bahamian owned platform for diverse learners.

Learning Resource Lab

Many students find it difficult to focus in a traditional classroom. This online learning platform will help your child to learn at their level with a variety of activities, content notes and differentiated instructions. Your child will have an unlimited access of class notes and videos tailored to the Ministry of Education's Curriculum. Also, parents can track their child's progress and communicate with teachers using the virtual chat button for guidance on homework, coursework, and school projects.
