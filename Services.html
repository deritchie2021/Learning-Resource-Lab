Our Services

Homework & Project Assistance


Connect with certified teachers for one on one assistance. This includes all subject areas from grades K5 to 12.

Topic Enhancement


Students will engage in virtual learning to aid further explanation of a topic from any subject area. There are one on one zoom classes available with certified teachers in each content area.

Glat/BJC/BGCSE Preparation


All worksheets are tailored to The Bahamas' National Examinations for Grades 3, 6, 9, 12. To prepare your child to pass these exams, utilize the Past Papers to help with their studies.

Reading Classes


Many children have difficulties blending letter sounds to form words. Our reading classes focuses on phonetic sounds, common words used, and reading assessments to reach your child's needs.

Lesson Plans


The Ministry of Education pacing guide is used to map out topics taught in the classroom. We have prepared readily available lesson plans for teachers to use for each topic and subject.

Content Notes & Videos


Students who are visual, auditory, and kinesthetic learners are equipped with lessons and notes on their learning level.
